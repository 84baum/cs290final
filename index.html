toneBox is a community supported site dedicated to the study of the sonic aging of musical instruments. Our passion is music and the development of tools to understand the process of musical instrument maturation and the tone we all associate with fine vintage instruments.

We decided to develop this site to allow not just makers of instruments, but owners as well, to archive and study the tonal aging of their instruments. We do this by maintaining user uploaded sound files and providing proprietary graphing and comparison tools that allow the user to track frequency response changes over time, overtone and fundamental decay and note balance.

Through this collaboration between instrument makers and consumers over time we hope to develop a knowledge base that will lead to a refinement of design choices my makers and an educated consumer base.
